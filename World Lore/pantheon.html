Aldmeri Dominion Pantheon

Collected within the Archive of RutausRP.

"Among the Aldmeri Dominion there are many faiths, but none are more widespread than the worship of the Eight."

The Pantheon of the Aldmeri Dominion is more than a collection of divine names. It is a living tradition, shaped by countless of victories, tragedies, and the people who chose to believe.

The Aldmeri Dominion divine goddesses maintain order around Tamriel. The pantheon consists of eight plus one goddesses.

The Pantheon of the Dominion: The eight

After countless of battles and many days at wars, the AD queen Elanwel'Snear decided to ask from goddess lady azura to help her create the Aldmeri Dominion Pantheon. A number of eight goddesses who will protect the citizens of the Dominion and maintain order around the regions. The pantheon is including: the Goddess of Love and Beauty Lady Azura, the Goddess of Mercy and Charity Elanwel'Snear, the Goddess of war Vex Landa, the Goddess of Work and trade Siloril Sositte, the Goddess of skies and lightnings Nerael'ruta, the Goddess of time Aranelis Calithrae, the Goddess of Life and Death Lathanwe'Silmaranis, and the Goddess of wisdom and Logic Keaniel Beeravi.

The ninth companion who ascended into the divine

When AD queen asked from lady Azrua to create the pantheon, Azura already had in mind a ninth goddess that will join them later when the time comes. Weeks before the request from AD queen, Azura met an imp who she had a vision about her that one day she will have an important role in the pantheon. After congquering the entire of tamriel and uniting everyone under 1 empire and flag -
the "Holy Cyrodiil Empire", Azura understood about her vision from before and understood what she needs to do now. Our new Empress who brought peace to tamriel has ascended to the divines and became the Goddess of wine and tits - Talriel Septa

The Divine Hierarchy

For the divines, no one is above the other. They all work together to make the lives of everyone else better. To help them with their jobs, every goddess gave at least one bosmer the powers of a Paladin - the right hand of the goddess. The paladins includes: Paladin of Goddess of Love and Beauty Aela Maëlle, Paladin of Goddess of Mercy and Charity Arrine'Belore, Paladin of Goddess of War Lirith Talioril, Paladin of Goddess of Work and Trade Aurica Invicta, Paladin of Goddess of Skies and Lightnings Aemilia Aquila, Paladin of Goddess of time Elanariel Redoran Junior, Paladin of Goddess of Life and Death Lirael'Selenar, Paladins of Goddess of Wisdom and Logic are two cuties.
