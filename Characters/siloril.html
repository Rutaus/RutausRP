General Information

Race: Bosmer

Class: Sorcerer

Role: Goddess

Order: AD pantheon

Home: Bosmer heaven

Biography

Siloril Sositte is the goddess of the hard Work and Trade. She is also one of the owners of the "Bosmer heaven" - a home for 30+ wild cutie bosmers.

Known Allies & Connections

  • AD Pantheon of the Eights: Siloril Sositte is part of the Aldmeri Dominion Pantheon ofgoddesses. Eight Goddesses who maintain order around tamriel.
  • Traveling Companions: The list is yet to be written.
  • Relatives and family: Siloril Sositte is Married to goddess of skies and lightnings Nerael'Ruta.
    Together with Nerael, they have 2 cutie daughters - Larveneth and Neravelle Aramyr.

Notable Events

  • Becoming the AD Intel Master: In her young, Siloril was a good friend with Elanwel'Snear. They used to sneak in many places together, pretending they are secret agents. When the time has arrived, Siloril helped her friend Elanwel to take down the queen with her intel job. From that day, Elanwel gave Siloril her title: AD Intel Master.
  • Meeting her wife: After Nerael'Ruta helped taking down Ayrenn from the throne of the dominion, the new queen and her friend - Elanwel - arranged a meeting between Siloril and Nerael. They fell in love and got married, in a - red wedding type of party.
  • Living her life: Some time after marrying her love Nerael, they had two daughters - Larvaneth and Neravelle.
    Together with her wife, they made a home for many bosmers who escaped the imperial camps. later they named their house: "Bosmer Heaven"
  • Ascending to the divines: For making lives for AD citizens better, AD queen decided to create the AD Pantheon. Siloril was chosen to become to goddess of the work and trade.
