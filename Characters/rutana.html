Biography

Rútana serves as the Vigil-Bearer of the Quiet Light, devoting her life to protecting others through discipline, compassion, and unwavering resolve.

Known Allies & Connections

  • Vigil of the Quiet Light: The order Rútana serves, bound to protection, healing, and quiet resolve.
  • Traveling Companions: In her days in cyrodiil, Rútana met with AD hero Nerael'Ruta and become her friend.
  • Relatives and family: Rútana have a twin sister named Aela Maëlle. Aela is a paladin for goddess of love and beauty - Lady Azura.
    In her journey, Rútana met her true love - Elanwell'snear, which later became the queen of summerset.
