Biography

Nerael'Ruta is the goddess of the skies and lightnings. She is also one of the owners of the "Bosmer heaven" - a home for 30+ wild cutie bosmers.

Known Allies & Connections

  • AD Pantheon of the Eights: Nerael'Ruta is part of the Aldmeri Dominion Pantheon of goddesses. Eight Goddesses who maintain order around tamriel.
  • Traveling Companions: The list is yet to be written.
  • Relatives and family: Nerael'Ruta is Married to goddess of work and trade Siloril Sositte.
    Together with siloril, they have 2 cutie daughters - Larveneth and Neravelle Aramyr.

Notable Events

  • Becoming the AD hero: After countless of battles in cyrodiil, and helping his friend Elanwel'snear becoming the AD queen, Nerael became an Aldmeri Dominion Hero.
  • Meeting her wife: After Nerael'Ruta helped taking down Ayrenn from the throne of the dominion, the new queen and her friend arranged a meeting between Nerael and Siloril. They fell in love and made a beautiful wedding, including a big massacre.
  • Living her life: Some time after marrying her love Siloril, they had two daughters - Larvaneth and Neravelle.
    Together with her wife, they made a home for many bosmers who escaped the imperial camps. later they named their house: "Bosmer Heaven"
  • Ascending to the divines: For making lives for AD citizens better, AD queen decided to create the AD Pantheon. Nerael was chosen to become to goddess of the skies and lightnings.
